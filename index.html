Rodent Facts

I've put here a few facts about rodents that I found really interesting
I hope you like them!

Hamster's cheeks

A golden hamster's cheek pouches extend all the way to its hips!

Saliva

Gerbils can recognize each other by the taste of their saliva

Communication

A gerbil may stomp its feet to communicate

Teeth

A rat's front teeth can grow up to 12.7 centimeters a year!

Baby mice

A baby mouse is called a pinky

Sleep

Guinea pigs often sleep with their eyes open
